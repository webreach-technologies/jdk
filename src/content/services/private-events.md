Private events deserve transportation that fits the occasion. JDK Transportation works with you
to plan pickup locations, stops, and timing so your group can focus on celebrating instead of
coordinating rides.

## A Plan Built Around Your Event

Whether your group is heading to one venue or making several stops throughout the day, we'll
build an itinerary around your schedule. Flexible pickup and drop-off locations make it easy for
everyone to get there together.

## Comfortable, Celebration-Ready Vehicles

Spacious seating and climate control keep your group comfortable, while ambient lighting and
onboard sound systems on select vehicles help set the mood for a celebration.

## Ideal For Any Occasion

From family reunions and milestone birthdays to concert outings and seasonal light tours, JDK
Transportation provides one vehicle and one driver for your whole group — no caravanning required.
