Planning a school trip means juggling schedules, headcounts, and safety expectations. JDK

Every vehicle in our fleet is regularly inspected, and every driver is held to the highest safety
standards. We work with school administrators and trip coordinators to plan routes, pickup times,
and drop-off logistics in advance.

Reclining seats, climate control, and onboard restrooms on larger buses keep students comfortable
on longer trips to museums, parks, and historic sites. Ample storage handles backpacks, lunches,
and class materials.

## Ideal For Schools and Parent Groups

From single-day field trips to multi-stop class excursions and end-of-year celebrations, our team
can match the right size vehicle to your group and itinerary.
