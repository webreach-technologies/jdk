Congregations and faith-based organizations rely on dependable transportation to keep members
connected — whether it's a weekly shuttle to services or a multi-day retreat. JDK Transportation
provides comfortable buses and consistent scheduling for groups of every size.

## Recurring Routes You Can Count On

We work with congregations to set up recurring shuttle routes for weekly services and regular
events, with consistent drivers and reliable scheduling.

## Comfortable for Retreats and Trips

Climate control, reclining seats, and ample storage make our buses well suited for multi-day
retreats, conferences, and youth group trips that require overnight bags and supplies.

## Ideal For Congregations of Every Size

From small youth groups to large congregations, JDK Transportation can match the right vehicle to
your group's size and travel schedule.

Request a quote today and our team will help plan transportation for your congregation's next
trip or event.
